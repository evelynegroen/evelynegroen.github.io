Introduction to uncertainty analysis and sensitivity analysis in environmental impact assessment models

Case study

All methods for uncertainty propagation and sensitivity analysis are applied to an (infamous) case study, first introduced by Heijungs [2002]. The case study respresents the production of electricity, but is a drastic simplification. Figure 1 shows a flow diagram of the case study.
