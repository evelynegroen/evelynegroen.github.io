About this website

Hi there! I am a PhD candidate at Wageningen University, the Netherlands. Currently I live in Berlin. My research interests are uncertainty analysis and sensitivity analysis in environmental modelling. Recently, I handed in my PhD thesis An uncertain climate: the value of uncertainty and sensitivity analysis in environmental impact assessment of food. On this website, you can find the introduction, discussion and summarys of my thesis.

I will use this website to distribute matlab code for performing uncertainty analysis and sensitivity analysis in environmental impact assessment models, which I used in some of my recent publications that were included in my PhD thesis.

Evelyne
