About this website

Hi there!

I am interested in data analysis, error propagation, uncertainty analysis and sensitivity analysis in mathematical modelling. For example, I am interested in the effect of correlation during uncertainty propagation on the uncertainty of model output. Recently, I handed in my PhD thesis An uncertain climate: the value of uncertainty and sensitivity analysis in environmental impact assessment of food. On this website, you can find the Introduction, Discussion and Summary of my thesis.

In addition, I will use this website to distribute MATLAB code for performing uncertainty analysis and sensitivity analysis in environmental impact assessment models, which were used in some of my recent publications.
