About this website

Hi there!

I am interested in data analysis, error propagation, uncertainty analysis and sensitivity analysis of mathematical models. Recently, I succesfully defended my PhD thesis An uncertain climate: the value of uncertainty and sensitivity analysis in environmental impact assessment of food at Wageningen University, the Netherlands. My PhD research focussed on the effect of correlation during uncertainty propagation on the variance of the model output. On this website, you can find the Introduction, Discussion and Summary of my thesis.

In addition, I will use this website to distribute MatLab and Python code for performing uncertainty analysis and sensitivity analysis, which were used in my publications.
